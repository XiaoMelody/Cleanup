  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE): Ensures continuous app functionality like navigation while the app is in the background.
  • Billing & License Verification (BILLING, CHECK_LICENSE): Used to verify in-app purchases and app licensing.
  • Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK): Used to keep the device's screen on while the app is running, ensuring uninterrupted service.
  • Bind Get Install Referrer Service (BIND_GET_INSTALL_REFERRER_SERVICE): Used to retrieve referrer information to help us understand where app installations are coming from.
  • Access Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE): To check the status of the network connection for proper app functioning.
  • Access Wifi State (ACCESS_WIFI_STATE): To determine the status of the Wi-Fi connection and optimize app performance based on the network type.

  • Use Fingerprint (USE_FINGERPRINT): For fingerprint authentication if applicable to certain app features.
  • Read Media Images (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES): Access images stored on your device for functionality such as uploading or editing photos.
  • Read Media Audio (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO): Access audio files on your device if required for specific app features.
  • Read Media Video (READ_MEDIA_VIDEO): Access video files on your device for features like uploading or editing videos.
  • Vibrate (VIBRATE): To make the device vibrate for notifications or alerts.
  • Internet (INTERNET): For internet connectivity to load external content and services in the app.

  • Access Adservices (ACCESS_ADSERVICES): Used for accessing ad services and attribution to optimize the ad experience.
  • Access Adservices Attribution (ACCESS_ADSERVICES_ATTRIBUTION): For tracking attribution information related to ads served in the app.
  • Access Adservices Ad Id (ACCESS_ADSERVICES_AD_ID): To collect ad-related identifiers to improve advertising and user experience.
  • Access Adservices Topics (ACCESS_ADSERVICES_TOPICS): For subscribing to and managing ad service topics for personalized ad targeting.
  • Nearby Wifi Devices: Used to detect available wifi networks for network-related features.
  • Change Wifi State: For changing the wifi network state on the device.
  • Change Wifi Multicast State: To modify wifi multicast state for networking features.
  • Bind Apphub Service: Used to integrate with Apphub service for app-related functionality.
  • Foreground Service System Exempted: Ensures continuous operation of background services without restrictions.
  • Bind Vpn Service: Used for integrating with VPN services.
  • Set Alarm: For scheduling alarms on your device.
  • Receive Boot Completed: Used to perform tasks when the device restarts.
  • Foreground Service Type Short Service: To manage short-running services in the foreground.
  • Schedule Exact Alarm: Used to schedule alarms with exact timing on your device.
  • System Alert Window: Allows the app to display system-level alert windows on top of other apps.
  • Package Usage Stats: For tracking the usage stats of installed apps.
  • Get Tasks: For accessing the tasks running on the device.
  • Some Permission: A placeholder for specific permissions that are not detailed.
  • Request Legacy External Storage: To access files from external storage on older devices or APIs.
  • Manage External Storage: Used to manage files and external storage operations.
